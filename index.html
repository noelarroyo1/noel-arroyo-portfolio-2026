ABOUT

Hello! My name is Noel Arroyo. I'm a Strategist, Researcher driven by curiosity and obsessed with outcomes. New projects and industry verticals don't intimidate me. I bring the same rigor to every engagement: asking the right questions, making data-informed decisions, and staying locked in on outcomes that make real business impact. Anyone can claim adaptability, but I believe it's my superpower and my portfolio makes the case for me.

What keeps me energized is the full arc of the work, from the messy early stages all the way to seeing real business results land. That process is where I live.

Outside of work, I'm a husband and father of three. I'm also a musician, multi-instrumentalist, and visual artist who finds creativity non-negotiable, whether I'm solving a new problem or picking up something unfamiliar. You might catch me hiking, exercising, building a mini-ITX PC, sketching sticker concepts, or programming music on my Synthstrom Deluge. I care deeply about what I make, and that passion shows up in everything I do.

I don't just deliver work. I deliver results you can point to.
